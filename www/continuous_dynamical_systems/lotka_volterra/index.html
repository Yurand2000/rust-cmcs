Systems of Recurrence Equations
Lotka-Volterra Predator-Prey

dV/dt = rV - aVP
dP/dt = -sP + abVP

where r > 0 is the birth rate of preys, r > 0 is the death rate of predators,
1 ≥ a ≥ 0 is the proportion of meetings resulting in a hunt, and b > 0 is the number of predator offsprings per hunt.
